Revolutionizing the Online Shopping Experience

The eCommerce landscape is evolving rapidly, and with it, customer expectations. Our 3D Virtual Trial Room represents a paradigm shift in how consumers interact with online fashion retailers.   • Launch and Optimization: Full-scale deployment and continuous improvement.

Addressing Online Shopping Challenges

Our 3D Virtual Trial Room tackles online fashion industry pain points, enhancing shopping confidence and reducing returns.

  • High Return Rates: Up to 40% of clothing purchased online is returned due to fit issues.
  • Customer Hesitation: 70% of cart abandonments are due to uncertainty about fit and appearance.
  • Retailer Costs: Returns cost retailers an average of $10-$20 per item.

Product Design

With a clear understanding of user needs, we designed an intuitive and engaging interface for our 3D Virtual Trial Room.

  • Avatar Customization: Users create a personalized 3D avatar by adjusting measurements, skin tone, and hairstyle.
  • Virtual Dressing Room: Mix and match outfits, adjust sizes, and view from multiple angles.
  • Outfit Comparison: Side-by-side view allows users to compare different outfits or sizes.

Technical Implementation

Using advanced AR and 3D technologies, we created a lifelike virtual try-on experience with real-time rendering.

  • 3D Scanning: High-resolution scans of clothing items using photogrammetry.
  • Avatar Generation: Machine learning models generate personalized avatars.
  • Cloth Simulation: Physics-based algorithms simulate how fabrics drape and move.
